About Me

Hello! I'm a Computer Science and Applied Math student at the University of California, Berkeley. I am an aspiring technical artist and tools developer passionate about empowering animators through technology. I aim to combine my rigorous CS training with my hands-on experience in art to explore how AI can augment -- not replace -- creative processes in film and game production

I love to spend my free time reading, painting, and exploring any form of storytelling, whether it's movies, music, books, or art. I'm especially fascinated by history, particularly the Victorian era, the Enlightenment, and the French Revolution (I love reading about Robespierre!)
